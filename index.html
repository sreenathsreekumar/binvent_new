BInvent 2020 – H&R Block’s First National Hackathon

BInvent 2020, pioneered as the First National Level Hackathon, is an initiative organized by H&R Block, powered by Team Block Inventors. The event is conducted with over 175 college participants from India. The elemental purpose of this event is to concatenate the idea and innovations of the fresh minds with the world of invention. Innovation- at its creative best, will remain the quintessence of the event, across its various levels.

BInvent 2020 was officially launched by Alan Lowden (Chief Information Officer, H&R Block) and Tiffany Monroe (Chief People Officer, H&R Block) on the 20th November 2019 during the All Hands Meet.

The event is charted to be a 3-month programme from XX Dec 2019 to 10th Feb 2020 over 3 exciting rounds. Team up and identify creative solutions to real problems, achieve results, cheer up and empower idea generation to lay the groundwork for crowning this challenge.

College participants shortlisted for BInvent 2020 will find themselves with a first-hand entry towards H&R Block Career and Internship opportunities.

AR for Students Education: Technical Round

Develop an Augmented Reality (AR)-based education application to teach and explain students in the most interesting way.

Teachers can use these app to catch students’ attention and help them to learn any topics (eg: science topics and experiments) by giving a realistic experience.

The developed AR-based application can be used by students to learn any topic including but not limited to; the entire universe, solar systems, and each planet in detail.

The developed application/app framework later can be adjusted to any domain with added features

Business Solution Contest for Management Students

Come up with a tax product solution that can disrupt (change) the tax business industry. Countries where tax is levied from the income generating citizens, there is also tax returns filing mechanism in place. Right now the solutions are provided as tax experts helping customers at tax offices, customers directly filing their tax returns through online tools etc… What could be the next generation tax solution that you can envision. Define the solution in the form of a 3-5 slides presentation (Microsoft Powerpoint Presentation). If your idea includes a software approach, pictures of the envisioned product (wireframes) will suffice. Prototype is not required.

Block Inventors

Block Inventors is a team at H&R Block formed to carve and support innovative and creative minds of the organization. The primary goal of the team is to develop a culture of innovation at the organization and pave a platform to support Idea Patenting and/or implementation of the same.

H&R BLOCK

H&R Block, Inc., or H&R Block is an American tax preparation company operating in North America, Australia, and India. The company was founded in 1955 by brothers Henry W. Bloch and Richard Bloch.

As of 2018, H&R Block operates approximately 12,000 retail tax offices staffed by tax professionals worldwide. It also offers consumer tax software as well as online tax preparation and electronic filing from their website. The Kansas City–based company also offers payroll, and business consulting services
